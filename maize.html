Tips for Maize

The crop can very quickly reach 20% moisture after it matures.
On average a 40 bu/ac wheat crop at maturity will contain approximately 95 kg/ha (85 lb/ac) of nitrogen in the seed and straw.
Lectins, phytic acid, digestive inhibitors, and various other antinutrients, wheat is mostly contaminated with mold toxins.
